1. Free Course Lead Magnet

Instagram Lead Capture

What It Does

When a follower comments "COURSE" on any of your posts, ManyChat automatically sends them a DM with a link to your free Financial Literacy course, then follows up 24 hours later.

Trigger: Instagram Comment contains keyword COURSE (case-insensitive) on any post
1
Trigger
User comments "COURSE" on any Instagram post
↓
2
Condition
Check if user is a new subscriber (hasn't received this before)
↓
3
Send DM
Deliver the course link
Hey! Thanks for your interest in leveling up your finances. Here's your free Finance With Phil course: [COURSE_LINK]

This covers budgeting, investing basics, and credit — everything you need to start building wealth. Let me know if you have any questions!
↓
4
Tag
Add tag: course_lead and ig_lead
↓
5
Smart Delay
Wait 24 hours
↓
6
Follow-Up DM
Re-engage if they haven't opened
Hey! Did you get a chance to check out the free course? Let me know if you have any questions — I'm here to help you on your wealth-building journey.
Pro Tip: Add a CTA in your post captions like "Comment COURSE to get my free financial literacy course!" This trains your audience to engage with keyword triggers.

ManyChat Setup Steps

  1. Go to Automation → New Flow → Start from Scratch
  2. Set trigger: "Instagram Comment" → Keyword "COURSE" → All Posts
  3. Add condition: "Is Subscriber" → If No, continue flow; If Yes, send "You already have access!"
  4. Add action: Send Message → paste DM copy above
  5. Add action: Add Tag → "course_lead", "ig_lead"
  6. Add Smart Delay: 24 hours
  7. Add action: Send Message → paste follow-up copy
  8. Publish flow

Instagram Sales

Turns interest into booked calls. When someone comments "CONSULT" or DMs asking about consulting, they get guided through a qualification funnel that ends with a Calendly booking link.

Quick Replies: A) Just getting started | B) Have some experience, want to grow | C) Ready to scale to the next level

ManyChat Setup Steps

  1. Go to Automation → New Flow
  2. Set trigger: Comment keyword "CONSULT" + DM keywords "consulting", "1-on-1", "session"
  3. Add Welcome Message with text above
  4. Add Quick Reply node with 4 options (Credit, Investing, Real Estate, Business)
  5. Add Quick Reply node with 3 experience levels
  6. Add Message with Calendly booking link
  7. Add Tags based on answers using Conditions
  8. Add Smart Delay: 48 hours

5. Welcome New Follower

Instagram Engagement

What It Does

Sends a personalized welcome sequence to every new follower, asks about their money goals, and delivers a relevant free resource based on their answer. This segments your audience from Day 1.

Trigger: New Instagram Follower (Note: requires Instagram Messaging API)
1
Trigger
New user follows @phillip.karaya
↓
2
Smart Delay
Wait 5 minutes (feels more natural)
↓
3
Welcome DM
Welcome to the Finance With Phil community! I help people build real wealth through smart money moves, investing, and entrepreneurship.

Quick question so I can point you to the right content...
↓
4
Quick Reply
What's your #1 money goal right now?
A) Save more money | B) Start investing | C) Start a business | D) Fix my credit
↓
5
Conditional Responses
Each answer leads to a different resource:
A) Save more → "Here's my free budgeting template: [LINK]"
B) Invest → "Check out my investing 101 guide: [LINK]"
C) Business → "Here's my side hustle starter guide: [LINK]"
D) Credit → "Here's my credit repair checklist: [LINK]"
↓
6
Tag
Tag based on answer: goal_savings, goal_investing, goal_business, or goal_credit
Pro Tip: These tags are gold for future campaigns. When you launch a credit course, blast everyone tagged goal_credit. When you promote your investing content, target goal_investing. Personalized outreach converts 3-5x better.

ManyChat Setup Steps

  1. Go to Automation → New Flow
  2. Set trigger: "New Follower" (requires Instagram messaging permissions)
  3. Add Smart Delay: 5 minutes
  4. Add Message: Welcome text
  5. Add Quick Reply: 4 options for money goals
  6. Add Condition for each answer → different message + different tag
  7. Publish flow
